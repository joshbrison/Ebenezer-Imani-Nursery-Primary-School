Fees Structure

Transparent and affordable fee structure for quality education

School Fees 2026 Academic Year

At Ebenezer Imani, we are committed to providing quality education at reasonable costs. Our fee structure is transparent with no hidden charges. All fees are payable per term (3 terms per year).

Important Notes for Day School

  • Admission fee is paid once when joining the school
  • Development fee is paid once per year (first term only)
  • Tuition fee includes: textbooks, exercise books, and basic stationery
  • Fees are payable at the beginning of each term
  • Late payment may attract a penalty of UGX 10,000 per week

Boarding Section Includes:

  • Accommodation in dormitories (separate for boys and girls)

Discounts & Scholarships

We offer various discounts to support our school community and recognize excellence.

Siblings Discount

Multiple Children Discount

Families with more than one child at our school receive:

  • 2nd child: 5% discount on tuition
  • 3rd child: 10% discount on tuition
  • 4th child+: 20% discount on tuition

Discount applies to tuition fee only. All other fees remain full.

Early Payment

Early Bird Discount

Pay your term fees early and save:

  • Payment 4 weeks before term: 5% discount
  • Payment 2 weeks before term: 3% discount
  • Full year payment in advance: 10% discount

Discount applies to total term fees (excluding one-time payments).

Academic Excellence

Merit Scholarships

Top performing students may qualify for:

  • Position 1 in class: 25% scholarship
  • Position 2 in class: 15% scholarship
  • Consistent performance: Various scholarships

Awarded based on previous term's results and conduct.

Special Consideration

Financial Assistance

Limited financial aid available for:

  • Orphans and vulnerable children
  • Children of single parents
  • Families with proven financial need
  • Children with special talents

Requires application and documentation. Limited slots available.
