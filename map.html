Transportation Options

Various ways to reach our school

Private Vehicle

Ample parking space available for parents and visitors. Secure parking area with 24/7 security surveillance.

School Bus Service

We provide school bus services covering major routes in Mukono and surrounding areas. Contact administration for routes and fees.

Public Transport

Regular taxi and boda-boda services available from Mukono town to Namayiba. Drop-off point is 500m from school.

How to Reach Us

Step-by-step directions from major locations

From Kampala City Center

1. Take Jinja Road heading east towards Mukono
2. Continue on Jinja Road for approximately 22km until you reach Mukono town
3. At Mukono roundabout, take Katosi Road (turn right)
4. Drive for 4 kilometers on Katosi Road
5. Look for the school signboard on your left at Kityabule - Namayiba
6. Turn left at the signboard and follow the road for 200m to reach the school

From Jinja Town

1. Take Kampala Road heading west
2. Pass through Lugazi town
3. Continue towards Mukono for approximately 35km
4. When you reach Mukono town, at the roundabout take Katosi Road (turn left)
5. Drive for 4 kilometers on Katosi Road
6. Look for the school signboard on your left at Kityabule - Namayiba
7. Turn left at the signboard to reach the school

From Entebbe International Airport

1. Take Entebbe-Kampala Road to Kampala
2. Once in Kampala, follow signs to Jinja Road
3. Take Jinja Road heading east towards Mukono
4. Continue for 22km to Mukono town
5. At Mukono roundabout, take Katosi Road (turn right)
6. Drive 4km to Kityabule - Namayiba
7. Follow the school signboard to your destination
