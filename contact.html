How to Reach Us

1

From Kampala City

Take Jinja Road towards Mukono. Pass through Mukono Town and continue on Katosi Road for approximately 4km. The school is in Kityabule - Namayiba, clearly marked with our signboard.

2

Using Public Transport

From Kampala, take a taxi/bus to Mukono Taxi Park. From Mukono, take a boda-boda (motorcycle taxi) or special hire taxi to Namayiba. Tell the driver "Ebenezer Imani School" - it's well known in the area.

3

By Private Vehicle

Use GPS coordinates: 0.3641° N, 32.7351° E. Ample parking space is available within the school compound for visitors.

Estimated Travel Times:

  • From Kampala: 45-60 minutes by car
  • From Mukono Town: 10-15 minutes by boda-boda
  • From Jinja: 40-50 minutes by car

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about contacting us.

What is the best time to visit the school?

The best time to visit is during our business hours: Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, or Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM. We recommend scheduling an appointment in advance, especially if you wish to meet specific staff members or take a school tour.

How long does it take to get a response to my inquiry?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For emails, you should receive a response within 1 business day. WhatsApp messages are typically answered within a few hours. Phone calls are answered during business hours. Urgent matters are prioritized.

Can I schedule a school tour?

Yes! We encourage prospective parents to tour our facilities. Please contact the school office via WhatsApp or phone call to schedule a tour. Tours are typically conducted on weekdays between 10:00 AM and 3:00 PM, but we can accommodate special arrangements if needed.
