Our Academic Structure

Following the Uganda National Curriculum with Christian values integration

Nursery Section (Age 3-6)

Our nursery section provides a nurturing environment for early childhood development through play-based learning and foundational skills.

Baby Class (Age 3-4)

Introduction to school environment, social skills, basic concepts

Middle Class (Age 4-5)

Pre-reading, pre-writing, numeracy skills, creative activities

Top Class (Age 5-6)

Reading readiness, number work, preparation for Primary One

Teaching Methodology

We employ diverse teaching methods to cater to different learning styles and ensure effective knowledge transfer.

Child-Centered Approach

Learning activities designed around children's interests and capabilities

Interactive Learning

Group work, discussions, and hands-on activities for active participation

Technology Integration

Use of computers and educational software to enhance learning

Differentiated Instruction

Tailoring teaching methods to meet individual student needs

Assessment System

We use a comprehensive assessment system to monitor student progress and provide feedback.

Continuous Assessment (60%)

  • Class exercises and assignments
  • Project work and presentations
  • Practical activities
  • Class participation
  • Weekly tests

Termly Examinations (40%)

  • End of term examinations
  • Mid-term tests
  • Standardized assessment tests

Progress Reporting
