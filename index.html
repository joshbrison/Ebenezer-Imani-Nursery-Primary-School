Welcome to Ebenezer Imani

Ebenezer Imani Nursery & Primary School is a Christian-founded institution committed to providing quality education grounded in Christian values. We offer both pre-primary and primary education with a focus on holistic development.

Our motto "Thriving on Unity" reflects our commitment to working together with parents, teachers, and the community to nurture responsible citizens.
