Welcome to Our Admissions Portal

We're delighted that you're considering Ebenezer Imani Nursery & Primary School for your child's education. Our admissions process is designed to be transparent, straightforward, and supportive.

Admissions are open for: Baby Class, Middle Class, Top Class (Nursery) and Primary One to Primary Seven

Admission Process & Timeline

1

Inquiry & School Visit

Contact us to schedule a school tour or request information. We encourage prospective parents to visit our campus to see our facilities and meet our staff.

Duration: Ongoing throughout the year

Contact: +256 761288927 or ebenezerimanischool@gmail.com

2

Application Submission

Submit the completed application form along with required documents. You can download the form below or collect it from the school office.

Deadline: Applications accepted throughout the year

Methods: Online submission, email, or in-person

3

Assessment & Interview

For Primary levels, students may undergo a simple assessment. Parents/guardians will have an interview with the Head Teacher.

Assessment: Basic literacy and numeracy skills

Interview: Discussion of school values and expectations

4

Admission Decision

You will receive an admission decision within 7 working days of assessment/interview. Successful applicants receive an admission letter.

Notification: Phone call followed by official letter

Next Steps: Fee payment and registration completion

5

Registration & Orientation

Complete fee payment and submit remaining documents. Attend orientation session for new students and parents.

Orientation: Introduction to school life and policies

Starting Date: As per academic calendar or agreed date

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the application deadline?

We accept applications throughout the year, but for placement at the beginning of the academic year (February), we recommend applying by December of the previous year. Late applications may be considered based on availability.

Is there an entrance exam?

For Nursery classes, there is no entrance exam. For Primary classes (P.2-P.7), we conduct a simple assessment to determine the child's current level and ensure appropriate placement. The assessment covers basic literacy and numeracy skills appropriate for the class level.

What are the school hours?

Day School: 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM (Monday to Friday)
Boarding Section: Full boarding with weekend arrangements
Extra-curricular activities are scheduled from 4:00 PM to 5:30 PM on selected days.

Do you offer scholarships or financial aid?

Yes, we offer limited scholarships and financial assistance to deserving students based on academic performance and financial need. Applications for scholarships are considered separately and require additional documentation. Please contact the administration office for more information.

Can my child join mid-term?

Yes, we accept mid-term admissions subject to availability of space. The tuition fees will be pro-rated based on the remaining weeks in the term. All other admission requirements and processes remain the same.

What is the teacher-student ratio?

We maintain a favorable teacher-student ratio to ensure quality education:
• Nursery: 1 teacher for every 15 students
• Lower Primary: 1 teacher for every 25 students
• Upper Primary: 1 teacher for every 30 students
Additional support staff are available for specialized subjects and activities.
