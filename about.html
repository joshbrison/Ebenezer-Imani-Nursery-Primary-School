Our History

Ebenezer Imani Nursery & Primary School was founded in 1995 as a Christian educational institution committed to providing quality education to children in Mukono and surrounding areas. The name "Ebenezer" means "Stone of Help" from the Bible (1 Samuel 7:12), and "Imani" means "Faith" in Swahili, reflecting our foundation in Christian faith.

Starting with just 35 students in a small building, we have grown to accommodate over 500 students from Nursery to Primary Seven. Our journey has been marked by God's faithfulness and the dedication of our staff, parents, and community.

Over the years, we have expanded our facilities, improved our academic programs, and established ourselves as one of the leading schools in Mukono District.
